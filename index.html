About Me

Hi, my name is Jon Beveridge, and I was born on the 6th of December. I decided to take the I.M.D." program to have a change of career. For the past ten years I've been working in the Ottawa area as a musician and specifically as a drummer. Music has been a passion of mine since I was fourteen but I decided I wanted a new challenge.

My brother had always directed me towards UX/UI design since he is a designer himself so I decided that it would be an interesting career path to take! I've always wanted to work more on the tech side and I think that this will be the perfect opportunity to further myself and the career I would like to have.
